常见问题

1. Solo HQ 是什么？

Solo HQ 是一款面向独立创业者、自由职业者、独立开发者、内容创作者和一人公司经营者的项目、任务与财务管理工具。

2. 如何登录？

Solo HQ 仅支持 Sign in with Apple。

3. 如何管理订阅？

你可以在 App 内进入会员页，点击“管理订阅”，或在 iOS 系统设置中的 Apple ID 订阅页面管理订阅。

4. 如何恢复购买？

进入会员页后点击“恢复购买”。

5. 会员价格是多少？

  • 月度会员：¥28 / 月
  • 年度会员：¥128 / 年
  • 终身会员：¥198

不同国家或地区的实际价格可能不同，最终价格以 App Store 显示为准。

6. 数据存储在哪里？

核心数据默认存储在你的设备本地。如果你开启 iCloud 同步，数据可能会通过 iCloud 在你的设备之间同步。

7. 如何删除数据？

你可以在 App 内删除项目、任务、财务记录、复盘内容和灵感记录。你也可以卸载 App 删除本地数据。

8. 小组件功能是否可用？

小组件功能将在后续版本开放。当前版本只显示即将上线说明。

FAQ

1. What is Solo HQ?

Solo HQ is a project, task, and finance management app for solo founders, freelancers, indie developers, creators, and one-person business operators.

2. How do I sign in?

Solo HQ only supports Sign in with Apple.

3. How do I manage my subscription?

You can go to the membership page inside the app and tap “Manage Subscription”, or manage it through your Apple ID subscription settings in iOS Settings.

4. How do I restore purchases?

Open the membership page and tap “Restore Purchases”.

5. What are the membership prices?

  • Monthly Plan: ¥28 / month
  • Yearly Plan: ¥128 / year
  • Lifetime Plan: ¥198

Actual prices may vary by country or region. The final price is shown by the App Store.

6. Where is my data stored?

Core data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable iCloud Sync, data may be synced across your devices through iCloud.

7. How do I delete my data?

You can delete projects, tasks, finance records, review content, and quick capture records inside the app. You can also uninstall the app to delete local data.

8. Are widgets available?

Widgets will be available in a future version. The current version only shows a Coming Soon message.
